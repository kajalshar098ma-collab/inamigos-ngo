About Our NGO

Who We Are

InAmigos Foundation is a youth-driven non-profit organization working to bring positive social change through impactful initiatives and community engagement.

Our mission is to create equal opportunities for underprivileged communities through education, awareness programs, food donation drives, environmental campaigns, and women empowerment.

We believe that small actions can create a big impact. With the support of volunteers and interns, we continue to work toward a better future.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Distribution of food, clothes, and essential items to needy and underprivileged people.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Supporting children's education through teaching programs, books, and learning activities.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development, awareness campaigns, and educational support.

Project JEEV

Promoting animal welfare and caring for stray animals through rescue and feeding drives.

Project PRAKRITI

Environmental protection through tree plantation, cleanliness campaigns, and awareness activities.

Project VIKAS

Helping youth build skills and gain practical experience through internships and workshops.
